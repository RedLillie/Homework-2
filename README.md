# Homework 2 — Process Improvement Suggester

## Business Workflow
This tool helps employees and managers submit notes about a current business process and receive structured, AI-generated suggestions for improvement.

**User**: Operations managers, team leads, or employees describing inefficiencies in their workflows.

**Input**: A plain-text description of a current business process and any pain points.

**Output**: A structured list of improvement suggestions with reasoning.

**Why automate?**: Process improvement analysis is time-consuming and often skipped. This tool makes it fast and repeatable, surfacing ideas that might otherwise be missed.
